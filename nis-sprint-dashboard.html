Figures are derived from the Azure DevOps board snapshot for the two-sprint period. Items are classified by board type — bug, task, or feature/epic — and grouped by product initiative. Near-duplicate entries were merged. Counts reflect the scope of work in the board for the period; individual done / in-progress status is not captured in this view. Bunny SDK = video delivery infrastructure; checkpoint / exit ticket refer to the Remedial School recovery flow; probation / academic watch is the at-risk student reporting feature.
